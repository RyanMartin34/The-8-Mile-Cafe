Mom's Spaghetti

Our number one classic recipe at the 8 Mile Cafe!

Grab a pen and paper, write down the required ingredients and enjoy yourself a meal that is guarenteed to make you vomit!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of Spaghetti
  • 1 Jar of Spaghetti Sauce
  • A sweater

Steps

  1. Place you spaghetti in a pan and cover with water
  2. Bring the water to a boil, then simmer for 12-15 minutes
  3. Drainer the water from the pan
  4. Empty the jar of spaghetti sauce into the pan of spaghetti and mix thouroughly
  5. Heat for a further 5 minutes then allow to cool
  6. Enjoy your completed Mom's Spaghetti
  7. Dont forget to vomit on your sweater!
