Cheddar Bob's Cheddar Cheese Pizza

Enjoy one of our best selling dishes from the comfort of your home!

This mouth watering pizza is certain to satisfy your taste buds but please do not shoot yourself in in the leg with your own gun!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of cheddar cheese
  • Mixed meat
  • 1 Jar of pizza sauce
  • Pizza dough

Steps

  1. Flatten out your pizza dough to the required consistancy
  2. Spread your pizza sauce on top of the pizza dough
  3. Place the mixed meat on top of the pizza
  4. Sprinkle your cheddar cheese on top of the pizza
  5. Place in the oven for 18 minutes at 180 degrees celcius
  6. Allow to cool
  7. Enjoy your pizza
