Papa Docs's Phili Steak

Phili steak toastie from the famous Papa Doc

If you want to join the 313 then you should try this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Toastie Bread
  • Cooked Shredded Steak
  • Phili Cheese

Steps

  1. Place your shredded steak on your toastie bread
  2. Put the desired amount of phili cheese on top of shredded steak
  3. Place the toastie bread and contents on an oven tray
  4. Place in the oven for 10 minutes at 180 degrees celcius
  5. Remove from oven and place toasted bread together
  6. Allow toastie to cool
  7. Eat and enjoy!
